p<>. Each year, at the first day of the conference we announce three best paper candidates selected based on the marks from the program committee. And at the end the program chair announces who is from the best paper candidates took 1st, 2d and 3d place. Below are the candidates and their places.

h2. 1st place

p<>. Aaaannnd...the first place in the Best Paper Competition goes to Claudia Bretschneider, Sonja Zillner and Heiner Oberkampfh from "University Munich":http://www.en.uni-muenchen.de/index.html, "Siemens AG":http://www.siemens.com and "Steinbeis University":http://www.steinbeis.de/en/  with the paper "UIMA2LOD: Integrating UIMA Text Annotations into the Linked Open Data Cloud"! Congratulations!
