p<>. KESW 2015 will be hosted on the main campus of the "Moscow Institute of Steel and Alloys":http://en.misis.ru/ within the walking distance from the Moscow's city center. It's right next to the famous "Gorky park":http://www.park-gorkogo.com/eng/about/, the Moscow's main centrally located place for recreation, sport, and outdoor activities. A subway ride to the Kremlin and the Red Square will take around 15 minutes.

p<>. The main University's building is located at the following address: Leninsky prospekt 4, 119049 Moscow. More details regarding the exact hall that will be used for the conference and how to get there will be available later.
