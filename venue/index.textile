p<>. KESW 2015 will be hosted by the "Russian Venture Company":http://www.rusventure.ru/en/ (RVC). RVC is a government foundation dedicated to development of venture investments in Russia
and financial support of research intensive industries.

p<>. The conference venue is expected to be the business center "Serebryany Gorod" on the bank of the Yauza river. The address is 29 Serebryanicheskaya nab., 109028, Moscow.
